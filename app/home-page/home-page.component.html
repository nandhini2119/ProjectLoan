Overview

Easy and flexible finance options for vehicles, ranging from two wheelers, cars, commercial vehicles, earth movers to farm equipment.

Eligibility

 Eligibility Salaried Individuals
  • Individuals who are minimum of 21 years of age at the time of applying for the loan, and not older than 60 at the end of the loan tenure
  • Individuals who have had a job for minimum 1 year and earning a minimum of Rs. 25,000 per month
 Self Employed Individuals
  • Individuals who are minimum of 21 years of age at the time of applying for the loan, and not older than 60 at the end of the loan tenure
  • Individuals who have had a job for minimum 1 year and earning a minimum of Rs. 25,000 per month

Documents Needed

KYC

Passport Size Photo

Bank Statement

Salary Slips or ITR
